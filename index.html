About Us

At StuCab, we are on a mission to revolutionize student commuting in Aberdeen by offering cheap, efficient, and eco-friendly transportation solutions. Our company was founded with a vision to address the transportation challenges faced by the student community while also contributing to the betterment of the local workforce and the environment.

  • Our Commitment to Sustainability:

    We firmly believe in the power of sustainable practices to create a greener, cleaner future. To achieve this, we have built our transportation service around electric vehicles (EVs). Our fleet of electrified KIA PICANTO cars replaces traditional gasoline-powered vehicles, significantly reducing carbon emissions and promoting eco-friendly travel throughout Aberdeen. Riding with StuCab means taking an active step towards supporting sustainable transportation options that benefit not just the student community but also the city as a whole.

  • Empowering the Aberdeen Community:

    As a locally-rooted business, StuCab takes pride in being deeply connected to the Aberdeen community. We are more than just a transportation service; we are an essential part of the social fabric that makes this city vibrant and thriving. Our job creation scheme aims to empower the local workforce by providing meaningful job opportunities. By hiring drivers locally, we contribute to the economic growth and well-being of Aberdeen.p>

  • Affordable Transportation for Students:

    Understanding the financial constraints that students often face, StuCab is dedicated to providing budget-friendly rides. We aim to be the go-to option for students seeking affordable transportation without compromising on quality or reliability. With exclusive student discounts and competitive fares, we ensure that students can travel conveniently without worrying about high costs.

Our Goals

Our Goals

Promote Sustainable Transportation:

Our primary goal is to encourage the adoption of sustainable transportation options in Aberdeen. By utilizing electric vehicles, we aim to significantly reduce carbon emissions and contribute to a greener and healthier environment for future generations.

Enhance Student Accessibility:

We strive to make transportation more accessible and affordable for students in Aberdeen. Our budget-friendly fares and exclusive student discounts ensure that every student can access convenient and economical rides to their desired destinations.

Optimize Commuting Experience:

StuCab is committed to providing a seamless and comfortable commuting experience for our users. Our progressive web app (PWA) streamlines the booking process, and our well-trained drivers prioritize safety and punctuality, ensuring a smooth journey from start to finish.

Contribute to Local Job Creation:

We aim to play an active role in the economic growth of Aberdeen by creating job opportunities within the community. By hiring drivers locally, we foster employment and contribute to the livelihoods of Aberdeen residents.

Build Community Engagement:

StuCab is more than just a transportation service; we aim to be an integral part of the Aberdeen community. Through partnerships with educational institutions and involvement in local initiatives, we seek to build stronger connections and foster community engagement.

Become the Preferred Student Transport Solution:

Ultimately, our goal is to become the go-to transportation service for students in Aberdeen. By offering reliable, eco-friendly, and cost-effective rides, we aspire to earn the trust and loyalty of our customers, making StuCab their preferred choice for daily commuting.
